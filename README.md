### Hey, I'm Kaif

I'm a Computer Science student at **Jamia Hamdard**, passionate about building **secure and interpretable AI**. My work focuses on creating tools that make generative AI more transparent and accessible, especially for public good. I have hands-on experience in full-stack development and applied machine learning, and I'm currently exploring **human-centered AI** and **agentic systems**.

Beyond coding, I enjoy **writing and photography**, always looking for new perspectives and ideas. I also enjoy prototyping experimental interfaces that reimagine human-computer interaction.

**I'm always happy to connect and collaborate on projects that use AI to make a real impact.**
